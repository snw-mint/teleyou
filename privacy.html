Privacy Policy

Effective January 2026

No data collection

TeleYou does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The tool runs entirely in your browser — images you upload and colors you pick never leave your device.

No tracking

TeleYou does not use cookies, analytics services, or any form of user tracking. There are no third-party scripts that monitor your behavior.

Local processing

All color extraction and theme generation happens client-side using WebAssembly and JavaScript. No server receives your images or generated themes.

External links

TeleYou links to GitHub and other external sites. Those sites have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/snw-mint/teleyou.
